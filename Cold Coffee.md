​                *Cold Coffee*

*INGRIDIENTS*

1)Milk(200ml)

2)Sugar

3)Coffee powder

4)Crushed ice or Cubed ice



​                                    *RECIPE*

1)Take the mixie

2)Add the Crushed ice or Cubed ice

3)Add 3tsp of sugar

4)Add 2tsp of coffee powder

5)Pour 200 ml milk into it

6)Mix it well in the mixie

7)Pour it in a glass 

​                         **YOUR COLD COFFEE IS DONE**
